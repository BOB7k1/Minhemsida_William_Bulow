Vision

Boots&Choose powers specialized online stores that enable customers and manufacturers to meet. We want to make trends and collective knowledge visible to make the production of quality products even better. We believe the customers' values in quality, price and environment will be crucial for future products.

Team Boots&Choose

Our philosophy is based on a desire to constantly learn new things and to share that learning. To accelerate competence and development speed, we work with crowdsourcing, AI and data-driven automation - tools that enable us to continuously improve our service together with customers and suppliers worldwide. From our office in Stockholm, or anywhere with an internet connection, we work to create the best possible experience.

Sustainability

E-commerce is the public transport of goods - smart, resource-efficient and sustainable. However, Boots&Choos’s ambition goes way beyond that. At the core of its business, strategy and vision, Boots&Choose is focused on sustainability and on transforming the industry into producing less waste and having lower error rates. By making thier large amount of data on consumer behaviour available to suppliers, Boots&Choose indirectly gives consumers impact on which products are produced and hence will be available in the future. Using the quick and clear feedback from from Boots&Choos’s platform in planning, product development and manufacturing gives suppliers and partners the opportunity to reduce returns, scrap and waste.

Carbon neutral air transport
